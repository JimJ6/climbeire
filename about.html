About Us

Climb Eire is a collective of rock-climbing enthusiasts. Our mission is to provide beginner rock climbers with the foundational knowledge it takes to build confidence and get started in rock-climbing. Our learn section is designed to consolidate all the requisite information to help newcomers gain a solid understanding by introducing the different types of rock-climbing as well as all the basic climbing terms. We also provide information and links to several climbing centres throughout Ireland so that beginners can take their acquired knowledge and put it into practice in the real world. We hope you will enjoy using our site and will find it useful. If you have any further questions you need answers to, please do not hesitate to get in touch below. If you are a climbing business and would like your information shared on our site, we would love to hear from you too. And please check out our resources page which is packed with tons of useful links to other websites, YouTube channels and more!
